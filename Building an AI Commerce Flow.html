Building an AI Commerce Flow

Transforming how customers shop through conversational AI

Project Overview

The Agentic Commerce Project is a strategic three-way partnership between URBN, Microsoft, and Stripe that enables customers to discover and purchase URBN products directly inside Microsoft Copilot’s conversational interface.

This positions URBN as an early mover in conversational commerce — a new sales channel alongside traditional e-commerce and retail.

The broader industry direction is clear: conversational journeys are converting, and the infrastructure is evolving to make merchant onboarding scalable (e.g., standards like ACP) and payments agent-friendly (e.g., shared payment tokens).

The Opportunity

Conversational commerce replaces search → click → website flows with natural dialogue, allowing customers to complete purchases without leaving the AI interface.

Partnership Structure

• Microsoft — Copilot interface & customer experience
• Stripe — Payments + Agentic Commerce Protocol (ACP)
• URBN — Merchant of record & fulfillment
